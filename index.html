Email Marketing Tool

Python, Django, Redis Queue, AWS SES, Bulma (CSS Framework), HTML/CSS

Contracted by an marketing company (now defunct) who wanted a tool to optimize the "email blasts" their employees sent. The company wanted their employees to write email marketing blasts to potential clients, with HTML formatted messaging in the email body.

The Django powered website I created allowed the employees to compose their message and enter the list of email addresses. Addresses would then be validated and queued for AWS SES to send the message. Statistics such as deliverability percentage would be shown to employees as their blast was processed, while management could supervise the efforts of their staff and different strategies. Additionally, the site allowed recipients of emails to permanently remove themselves from any further blasts.

The company's primary motivation with the project was to avoid the costs that similar platforms charge, while having data on the performance of each message and employee.
